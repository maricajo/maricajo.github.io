Meet Marica

Meet Marica

Marica has been practicing art all her life. From home decor to costume design to community art to fine art, she is involved in a little of everything. Former curator at Raging Rapid Art Gallery, she has also acted in Community Theatre. She paints trees on the walls of every house she lives in. With a strong love for the whimsical, she also paints portraits, murals, statues, decks, patios, and porches. Literally anywhere there is a "blank canvas", she will fill it with extraordinary life and color.
